Decentralised Database

Designed interfaces for BigchainDB — the first blockchain database. Making decentralized data infrastructure accessible to developers and enterprises during Berlin's Web3 emergence.

My Role
UX/UI Designer
Company
BigchainDB / IPDB Foundation
Duration
3 Years
Period
July 2015 — May 2018

Overview

BigchainDB was the first blockchain database — a decentralized infrastructure that combined blockchain benefits with database scalability. Developers and enterprises needed to understand how to query, store, and manage decentralized data — concepts that were novel and technically dense.

I worked across BigchainDB and the IPDB Foundation to design interfaces, documentation experiences, and product demos that communicated technical innovation clearly to investors and early adopters.

The Challenge

Blockchain databases were unprecedented. There was no established UX pattern for querying decentralized data, visualizing transaction flows, or onboarding developers to the concept. The challenge was to explain "blockchain meets database" to technical and non-technical stakeholders alike — without losing the precision that engineers needed.

Infrastructure UX requires clarity for both executives evaluating adoption and developers implementing the technology. Design had to serve both audiences.

Process & Impact

I designed and launched this product from concept to production as one of three major products during my time in the Web3 ecosystem. I created pitch decks, product demos, and investor-facing materials that communicated technical innovation clearly and helped secure funding rounds.

I established brand guidelines for BigchainDB and IPDB, ensuring visual consistency as organizations scaled. I facilitated design thinking workshops with cross-functional teams to explore innovative solutions for unprecedented product challenges.

Reception

The interfaces made complex blockchain database concepts accessible and engaging. The work impressed stakeholders and contributed to BigchainDB's early growth and the broader understanding of decentralized data infrastructure.
