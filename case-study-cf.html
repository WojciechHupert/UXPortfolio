Education UX/UI Mentorship Design Strategy

Empowering the Next Generation

Mentoring over 100 students globally through a rigorous UX/UI design bootcamp — guiding them from foundational research to high-fidelity prototyping and professional portfolio strategy.

My Role
UX/UI Design Mentor
Company
CareerFoundry (Remote)
Period
2014 — 2021

The Mission

CareerFoundry is one of the world's leading platforms for online design education. As a mentor, my responsibility went beyond just "grading papers." I acted as a professional guide, helping students navigate the transition from curiosity to career-readiness.

During my seven-year tenure, I mentored over 100 students, reviewing thousands of submissions and conducting countless deep-dive sessions onto UX research, information architecture, and visual design systems.

A Curriculum of Curation

I guided students through the complete product lifecycle:

  • 01 User Research & Analysis: Teaching how to conduct empathetic interviews, synthesis data, and create actionable personas.
  • 02 Architecture & Flows: Mapping out complex user journeys and ensuring navigation logic remains intuitive.
  • 03 Interface Design: Mastering the principles of hierarchy, typography, and color to create premium digital experiences.
  • 04 Portfolio Strategy: Helping students frame their process as a compelling narrative for hiring managers.

Impact & Reflection

Many of my former students are now working as Senior Designers at companies like Google, Spotify, and Amazon. This experience sharpened my own ability to communicate design decisions, critique work constructively, and stay at the absolute forefront of industry tools (Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD) and methodologies.

"Teaching is the best way to master a craft. Explaining complex UX principles to a beginner tests your own deep understanding of the 'Why' behind every pixel."
