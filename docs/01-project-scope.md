# Project Scope

## Project

- **Name:** Shape Seeds UX Portfolio
- **Type:** Static marketing and portfolio website
- **Primary audience:** Founders, operators, and teams exploring AI experience strategy
- **Current stage:** Active iteration

## Problem Statement

Potential clients need to quickly understand the value of AI Experience Design and trust the service enough to start a conversation.

## Desired Outcomes

- Communicate a differentiated positioning in under 30 seconds.
- Showcase selected work and credibility clearly.
- Convert qualified visitors to contact inquiries.

## In Scope

- Core marketing pages (`index`, `services`, `about`, `contact`).
- Portfolio and case study pages.
- Content clarity and messaging hierarchy.
- Visual consistency across pages.
- Performance and responsiveness improvements.
- Basic interaction polish in `script.js`.

## Out of Scope (for now)

- CMS integration or backend admin panel.
- User accounts and authentication.
- E-commerce or payment flows.
- Complex analytics dashboards.
- Full multilingual localization.

## Constraints

- Frontend-only stack (HTML/CSS/JavaScript).
- Keep hosting-simple deployment model.
- Preserve fast load and maintainable structure.
