# Project Aims and Success Metrics

## Vision

Build a high-trust portfolio experience that clearly positions Shape Seeds as an expert in AI Experience Design and generates qualified client conversations.

## Strategic Aims

## 1) Positioning Clarity

- Make the value proposition understandable within the first viewport.
- Clarify the "AI Experience Debt" concept with concise supporting copy.

### Indicators

- Visitor can summarize offer in one sentence during user feedback sessions.
- Lower bounce behavior on key entry pages.

## 2) Proof and Credibility

- Improve case study storytelling and evidence quality.
- Show practical outcomes (before/after, process, impact).

### Indicators

- Increased clicks from homepage to case studies.
- Improved average engagement depth on portfolio pages.

## 3) Conversion Quality

- Drive relevant inbound contact through clear calls to action.
- Reduce friction in contact flow and inquiry framing.

### Indicators

- More qualified contact submissions per month.
- Better conversion rate from service-page visitors to contact visits.

- Case studies remain visually strong but outcome-light.
- CTA overload causes decision paralysis.
