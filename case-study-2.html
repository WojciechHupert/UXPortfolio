FNZ Identity Verification

FNZ provides wealth management technology to financial institutions globally. I focused on identity verification systems used by banks to onboard customers — a critical workflow where friction directly impacts conversion and compliance.

Overview

FNZ provides wealth management technology to financial institutions globally. I focused on identity verification systems used by banks to onboard customers. This is a critical workflow where friction directly impacts conversion and compliance — every extra step or confusing instruction increases drop-off and regulatory risk.

I redesigned identity verification flows for mobile and desktop, significantly reducing user drop-off rates through improved guidance and clearer user feedback at each verification step.

Platform Overview — Core Modules

The Challenge

Identity verification is a high-friction moment in every banking relationship. Users must submit documents, complete checks, and wait for approval — often without clear feedback on progress or what to do if something fails.

The challenge was to reduce drop-off without compromising regulatory requirements. I needed to design flows that guided users step-by-step, provided clear feedback at each stage, and handled edge cases gracefully — while giving compliance officers the tools they needed to review and manage verification records.

In ID&V design, every unclear instruction is a conversion leak. The goal is to make the process feel guided and transparent, not bureaucratic.

Process & Collaboration

I created intuitive interfaces for compliance officers to review and manage verification records, meeting stakeholder requirements and achieving project goals with positive feedback.

I developed wireframes and interactive prototypes in Figma that effectively communicated complex verification flows to developers and stakeholders, ensuring accurate implementation while meeting WCAG accessibility standards.

Identity Verification Flows — Wireframes & Prototypes

Key Design Decisions

I designed step-by-step flows with clear progress indicators and immediate feedback at each verification stage. When a document was rejected or a step failed, users received specific guidance on what to correct — reducing support tickets and repeat attempts.

ID&V Flow Designs — Mobile & Desktop

The compliance officer interface prioritized searchability, filtering, and bulk actions — allowing teams to process verifications efficiently while maintaining audit trails and regulatory visibility.

Compliance Officer Interface — Review & Management

Outcome & Learnings

The redesigned flows were implemented and adopted by FNZ's financial institution clients. The improved guidance and feedback loops significantly reduced user drop-off while maintaining strict compliance with regulatory requirements.

Note: This position ended in August 2023 due to company restructuring and team redundancies. The work demonstrated my ability to design for high-stakes, regulated domains where conversion and compliance are equally critical — and where every unclear step has a measurable cost.
