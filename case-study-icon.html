Precision & Compliance

Designing Case Report Forms (CRFs) for international clinical trials — ensuring flawless data accuracy and full compliance with FDA and international healthcare regulations.

My Role
Technical Writer / Designer
Company
ICON Clinical Research (Dublin)
Period
2005 — 2006

The Stakes

ICON is a world leader in clinical research for the pharmaceutical industry. In this role, the stakes were incredibly high: even a minor design error on a data entry form could invalidate months of research or cause regulatory failures.

This was my first major exposure to highly regulated design environments — a foundation that has proven invaluable in my later work in FinTech and Web3.

Cognitive Load & Accuracy

I designed the Case Report Forms (CRFs) used by doctors and researchers during clinical trials. The objective was 100% data integrity.

"UX wasn't a buzzword then, but the principles were the same: reduce cognitive load for the researcher so they can enter medical data without error. Every input field was a potential point of failure."
